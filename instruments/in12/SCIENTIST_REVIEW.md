# IN12 Scientist Review

Please write comments anywhere. You do not need to fill out a schema or know
TAVI; a correction, drawing, or link is enough for maintainers to process.

The five questions that unblock a first model are:

1. Which present-day IN12 configuration should TAVI represent, including any
   analyser or multiplex upgrades since the retained 2016 paper?
2. What are the four TAS arm lengths, axis senses, and mechanical limits?
3. Which current source/guide sections should be simulated explicitly, and
   what is the virtual-source aperture and spectrum at the model boundary?
4. What are the current monochromator, analyser, collimator, slit, filter, and
   detector specifications?
5. Can you provide a few trusted elastic and inelastic angle readbacks for
   sign and geometry verification?
